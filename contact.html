Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure with HealthSync?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and are fully HIPAA compliant. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and you maintain full control over who can access it.

How do I connect my hospital records?

We partner with major healthcare providers and EHR systems. You can authorize HealthSync to access your records through secure, standardized APIs.

Can I share specific data with different providers?

Absolutely. You have granular control over what data each provider can see and for how long. You can revoke access at any time.

What happens if I change healthcare providers?

Your data stays with you. You can easily connect your new provider and share relevant health history without starting from scratch.
