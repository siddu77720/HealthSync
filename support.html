Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure with HealthSync? +

Yes, HealthSync uses bank-level encryption and is fully HIPAA compliant. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never store your personal health information in plain text. You maintain full control over who can access your data and can revoke permissions at any time.

How do I connect my hospital records to HealthSync? +

We partner with major healthcare providers and EHR systems. You can authorize HealthSync to access your records through secure, standardized APIs. Simply go to your dashboard, click "Add Data Source," select your healthcare provider, and follow the authorization process. Most major hospital systems are already integrated.

Can I share specific parts of my health data with different providers? +

Absolutely! HealthSync gives you granular control over data sharing. You can choose exactly which data categories each provider can access, set time limits on their access, and even create custom data views for different healthcare professionals. For example, you might share only relevant cardiac data with your cardiologist while sharing medication history with your primary care physician.

What happens to my data if I stop using HealthSync? +

You always own your data. If you decide to stop using HealthSync, you can export all your health data in standard formats (JSON, CSV, PDF) before closing your account. We will permanently delete all your data from our servers within 30 days of account closure, in compliance with data protection regulations.

How does HealthSync handle emergency situations? +

HealthSync includes emergency features that allow you to share critical health information quickly. You can set up emergency contacts, create an emergency health profile with allergies, medications, and conditions, and even generate a QR code that emergency responders can scan to access your vital information when needed.
