Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure with HealthSync in India? +

Yes, HealthSync uses bank-level encryption and complies with Indian data protection laws. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never store your personal health information in plain text. We have servers located in India to ensure compliance with local regulations.

How do I connect my hospital records from Apollo, Fortis, or other Indian hospitals? +

We partner with major Indian healthcare providers including Apollo, Fortis, Max, AIIMS, and many others. You can authorize HealthSync to access your records through secure, standardized APIs. Simply go to your dashboard, click "Add Data Source," select your healthcare provider from our list of Indian hospitals, and follow the authorization process.

Can I integrate my AYUSH treatment records with modern medical data? +

Absolutely! HealthSync supports integration with Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy (AYUSH) treatments. You can manually enter your AYUSH treatment records or connect with registered AYUSH practitioners who use our system. This gives your doctors a complete picture of all treatments you're receiving.

What happens if I move to a different city in India? +

Your HealthSync data travels with you! When you move to a new city, you can easily connect with local healthcare providers there. Your complete medical history from previous cities remains accessible, so new doctors have your full background without you having to carry physical files or repeat tests.

How does HealthSync handle emergency situations in India? +

HealthSync includes emergency features tailored for India. You can set up emergency contacts, create an emergency health profile with critical information in local languages, and generate a QR code that Indian emergency responders can scan. We also integrate with major ambulance services and emergency numbers like 108 and 102.

Is HealthSync available in regional Indian languages? +

Yes! HealthSync is available in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, and Malayalam. You can switch languages anytime from the navigation menu. Our support team also provides assistance in these regional languages.
