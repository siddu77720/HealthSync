Frequently Asked Questions

üîç

Is my health data secure with HealthSync? +

Yes, HealthSync uses bank-level encryption and complies with data protection laws. Your data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never store your personal health information in plain text.

How do I connect my hospital records? +

We partner with major healthcare providers. You can authorize HealthSync to access your records through secure, standardized APIs. Simply go to your dashboard, click "Add Data Source," select your healthcare provider, and follow the authorization process.

Can I integrate my AYUSH treatment records? +

Absolutely! HealthSync supports integration with Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy (AYUSH) treatments. You can manually enter your AYUSH treatment records or connect with registered practitioners.

What happens if I move to a different city? +

Your HealthSync data travels with you! When you move to a new city, you can easily connect with local healthcare providers there. Your complete medical history remains accessible.

How does HealthSync handle emergency situations? +

HealthSync includes emergency features that allow you to share critical health information quickly. You can set up emergency contacts, create an emergency health profile, and generate a QR code that emergency responders can scan.

Is HealthSync available in regional languages? +

Yes! HealthSync is available in multiple regional languages. You can switch languages anytime from the navigation menu.
